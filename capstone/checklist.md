# Capstone Completion Checklist

Use this checklist to confirm all 3 capstone artifacts are complete before marking the course done.

---

## Artifact 1: `capstone/roadmap.md`

**Section 1 — Current state self-placement**

- [ ] All 4 levels have an evidence statement.
- [ ] Every evidence statement is specific — references a committed file, a checklist, or an observable behavior.
- [ ] No section says "I feel comfortable" or any other self-impression without artifact backing.

**Section 2 — 90-day roadmap**

- [ ] All 4 time horizons are filled in.
- [ ] Each horizon has a gate deliverable that is a specific file or checklist item.
- [ ] Day 7 tasks are achievable at my current skill level — I have not front-loaded advanced tasks.
- [ ] 90-day gate references `capstone/roadmap.md` complete and `checklists/expert-completion.md` reviewed.

**Section 3 — KPI selection**

- [ ] Exactly 3 KPIs are named.
- [ ] All 3 are from the module's minimum set (checklist completion rate, session quality score, convention drift count).
- [ ] Every rejected metric has a one-sentence explanation.

**Section 4 — Governance policy**

- [ ] Policy tier is selected and matches my current team size.
- [ ] At least 5 rules are written in declarative format.
- [ ] Policy is committed to `CONVENTIONS.md` (team context) or recorded in `capstone/roadmap.md` (solo context).

**Section 5 — Review cycle commitment**

- [ ] Monthly review date is stated specifically (not "sometime each month").
- [ ] All 4 review cycle steps have a named responsible person.
- [ ] I understand what evidence each step requires.

**Artifact completeness**

- [ ] No `[your evidence]`, `[your focus]`, or `[specific file]` stubs remain.
- [ ] `capstone/roadmap.md` is committed to the repository.

---

## Artifact 2: `checklists/adoption-milestones.md`

- [ ] All milestone rows are filled in with my actual current state.
- [ ] Rows I have not yet reached are marked clearly as "Not yet" — not left blank.
- [ ] The document is committed to the repository.

---

## Artifact 3: `checklists/expert-completion.md`

- [ ] Every item in the expert completion checklist has been reviewed.
- [ ] Every item I can honestly check is checked.
- [ ] Every item I cannot check has a one-line remediation note.
- [ ] No items are left blank.
- [ ] The document is committed to the repository with a date note at the bottom.

---

## Final Verification

Before marking the capstone complete, verify:

- [ ] All 3 artifact files exist in the repository (`capstone/roadmap.md`, `checklists/adoption-milestones.md`, `checklists/expert-completion.md`)
- [ ] All 3 files are committed — not just saved locally
- [ ] The After Capstone section of `modules/10-adoption-roadmap/checklist.md` is fully checked
